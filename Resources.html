LAB TOOLS

  • BLOBULATOR: A webserver for protein blobulation. Blobulation is an approach for edge-detection in protein sequences based on contiguous hydrophobicity, originally developed by the Brannigan Lab.

  • SAFEP: A framework that computes absolute binding affinities by Streamlined Alchemical Free Energy Perturbation.

  • NOUGAT: A toolkit, developed by the Brannigan Lab, for the analysis of membrane disruption by proteins and other inclusions.
